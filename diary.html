Entry 04

Birthday Celebration with my friends

Today was such a great day. We all went out to celebrate my friend's birthday and honestly, it was one of those nights I didn't want to end. We headed to Wing Dynasty and went all out on their unli chicken wings — we were eating, laughing, and just enjoying each other's company the whole time. At some point we grabbed the photobooth and took a bunch of silly pictures together. Looking at those photos now, I can't help but smile. These are the kinds of moments that make college life truly special — not the exams or the deadlines, but the people and the memories we make along the way. Happy birthday to my friend Shiela, this one's for you!
